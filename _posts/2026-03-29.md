# Easy to say, hard to do

This was always my biggest fear when getting back into working on committing work on a more regularly basis.
I keep running into instances, school/work/life, where I dont have the time and instead would rather just
relax on the couch than help better myself. While on occasion, this is a healthy choice, when its nearly two months
between posts, that is when there is a problem. There has been a lot that has been going on lately, and that isnt an
excuse. I know that working to refine my skills and get ideas out is something that I need to work more on and I have
been not only aware of that fact, but the one that I have been neglecting. Clearly I finally got around to getting back
to posting, but I plan to make more of an effort. I have some ideas of what I want to work on and what I want to
research. I may not be commiting code daily, but I will be exercising and getting the reps in other ways. This is just 
a reminder to those in the same postiion, its ok that you stumble. Its being aware to get back up thats really important.
